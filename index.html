Need to pick a winner, but can't find a hat or pieces of scrap paper? Have no fear! Enter the classic carnival-style pony race. Give each person a pony (or more than one) and set those ponies racing. We don't have water guns to shoot or balls to roll, so your fate rests in the hands on a random number generator!
